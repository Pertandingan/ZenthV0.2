The Story of Zenth

Zenth is a secret language devised centuries ago by a group of scholars from the hidden city of Vethara. Its glyphs were designed to be unreadable to outsiders, carrying meaning only to those who mastered the script.

Originally used to encode messages, Zenth evolved into a fully transliterable alphabet, capable of writing any known language in a single, elegant system. Scholars say Zenth can reveal hidden patterns in ordinary words if studied closely.
